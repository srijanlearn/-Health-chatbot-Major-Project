# HealthGuard Insurance Policy 2026

## 1. General Coverage
HealthGuard provides comprehensive coverage for all premium members.
- Annual Deductible: $500
- Out-of-pocket Maximum: $3,000

## 2. Maternity Benefits
- Prenatal care is covered at 100% with no deductible.
- Delivery and hospital stays are covered at 80% after the deductible is met.

## 3. Dental and Vision
- Dental cleanings are covered twice a year.
- Vision exams are covered once a year. Glasses are covered up to $150.

## 4. Emergency Services
- Ambulance services are covered at 90%.
- Emergency room visits have a flat copay of $200, which is waived if the patient is admitted to the hospital.
